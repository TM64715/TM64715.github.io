About Me

As a web developer, I focus more on the Front-End of a site. This means design, user experience and user interface. I am more than capable of making a website for your small business or your blog and can include you as much or as little in the process as you would like. For the Back-End I am less comfortable but can still use PHP to create a personal experience for each user and perform, smaller server-side tasks.
